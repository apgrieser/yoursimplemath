About

Hi, I'm the creator behind Your Simple Math! With years of experience teaching high school math in public schools, I know firsthand the anxiety and frustration students often bring to the classroom.

I understand that a one-size-fits-all approach leaves some students bored and others completely overwhelmed. That's why I am so passionate about differentiation. My goal is to create high-quality resources that help you meet every student at their current skill level, allowing them to build confidence, conquer their math fears, and experience those magical "lightbulb" moments.
